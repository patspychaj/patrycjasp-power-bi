05 About

I'm a data analyst and Power BI developer based in Warsaw. I've worked across retail, finance, logistics, and HR — mostly building reporting infrastructure that didn't exist yet.

My focus is on the technical side: data modelling, DAX, and making sure the numbers are actually correct before worrying about how they look. That said, I care about how dashboards are structured and presented — a report that's hard to read is a report that doesn't get used.

This portfolio is a live document. I add things when they're finished and worth sharing, which means it's always slightly behind what I'm currently working on.
